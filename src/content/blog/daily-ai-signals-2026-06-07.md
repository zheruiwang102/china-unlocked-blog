# China Signals Daily - 2026-06-07

**Editor's Note:** Today's signals are overwhelmingly dominated by the annual *gaokao* (college entrance exam), which kicked off across China. The exam's difficulty, especially in math, and the essay prompts are the top trending topics, reflecting the immense social and familial weight of this single-day event. We also see a mix of lighter cultural moments, from a reality show ranking to a playful warning about replying to aliens.

---

## China Exam Signal: 高考数学难不难 (Is the Gaokao Math Exam Hard?)

**What happened:** "Is the gaokao math exam hard?" became the top trending topic on Douyin, with over 10.9 million views, as students emerged from the first day's math test. The question reflects a national ritual where exam difficulty becomes a shared, real-time social experience.

**Why it matters outside China:** The gaokao is more than a test; it's a high-stakes determinant of social mobility and family honor. The intense public discussion around its difficulty reveals the immense pressure on Chinese youth and how a single exam shapes life trajectories. For global observers, it's a window into China's competitive education system and the societal expectations placed on the "only child" generation.

**Watch next:** Look for viral posts from students and teachers analyzing specific math problems, which often become memes or points of national pride.
